Privacy Policy for Cosmos Connect

Effective Date: February 13, 2026

At Cosmos Connect, we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and handle your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal data to ensure the game functions correctly and to provide a personalized experience.

  • Game Progress & Achievements: If you sign in with Apple Game Center, we collect your player ID, high scores, and unlocked achievements to sync your progress across devices.
  • In-App Purchase Data: We do not collect your credit card info. All transactions are handled by Apple. We only receive confirmation that a purchase (e.g., "Remove Ads") was successful to unlock features for you.
  • Device Information: We may collect basic technical data (device model, OS version) to help us troubleshoot bugs.

2. Third-Party Services

To keep the game free, we use third-party tools that may collect data according to their own policies:

  • Google AdMob: Used to show ads. AdMob may use your device’s advertising ID to show relevant ads. You can opt out of personalized ads in your iOS device settings.
  • Apple Game Center: Used for leaderboards and cloud saves. Please refer to Apple’s Privacy Policy for more details.
