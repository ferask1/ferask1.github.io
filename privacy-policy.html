Privacy Policy for Cosmos Connect

Effective Date: March 16, 2026

At Cosmos Connect, we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and handle your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal data to ensure the game functions correctly and to provide a high-quality experience.

  • Game Progress & Syncing: If you use Apple Sign-In or Game Center, we collect your player ID and unlocked achievements to sync your progress across devices.
  • In-App Purchase Data: All financial transactions are handled securely by Apple. We use RevenueCat to verify that a purchase (e.g., "$2.99 Premium") was successful to unlock features. We do not see or store your credit card information.
  • Device & Location Data: We access your device's advertising ID (IDFA) and coarse location (IP-based) solely to provide localized and relevant advertisements via AdMob.

2. Third-Party Services

To support the game and provide premium features, we use third-party tools that process data according to their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob: Used to display advertisements. AdMob may use your advertising ID and coarse location to serve personalized ads. You can manage these preferences in your iOS "Tracking" settings.
  • RevenueCat: Used to manage in-app purchase receipts and verify your "Premium" status.
  • Apple Services: Used for cloud saves, leaderboards, and secure authentication.

3. How We Use Data

We use the information collected solely to:

  • Provide and maintain game functionality.
  • Process and restore in-app purchases.
  • Display relevant advertisements to keep the game free to play.
  • Monitor app performance and fix technical bugs.

4. Data Deletion & Your Rights

We respect your right to control your data. Since we do not maintain a private user database, your data is primarily stored within Apple's ecosystem or managed by the third-party SDKs mentioned above.
